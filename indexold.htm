National Stereoscopic Association '99 Convention Photos

I used my Sony 8mm Video Camera at the recent National Stereoscopic Association (NSA) Convention in Green Bay, WI and then digitized the pictures below with a Snappy video grabber.

Included among the photos below are pictures of the NSA trade show, workshops and stereo theater as well as some nearby Green Bay attractions.

Click (or double click) on any of the pictures or file names below for a BIGGER picture.
